Perfect!

A whole number that is equal to the sum of its positive factors (except the number itself). Example: 28 (positive factors are 1,2,4,7,14,28) is a Perfect number, because 1 + 2 + 4 + 7 + 14 = 28.

Even & Odd: Any integer that can be divided exactly by 2 is an even number.
